Who I am and What This is

This is a website that I set up for myself so that I could have a place to show off my many interests and projects. I felt that it would be beneficial to document all of the projects that I undertake both so I can see my process if I go back to a project and so that I can display my work to potential employers.In the links above I have included a link to a PDF of my resume as well as a link to my Projects page. There you can see photos and explanation on various projects I have completed both in school and on my own time. I always strive to keep pushing myself and expanding my knowledge, whether it is in the classroom or at an internship. This page is a manifestation of this desire to learn.

My name is Matthew Elia and I am Electrical Engineering major at Northeastern University. To date I have completed my first experiential education experience with a 6 month internship at Philips Color Kinetics, a architectual LED lighting company within Philips Lighting as well as a second internship at Emphysys Technology Solutions , a consulting firm that specializes in product development of RF Generators with a particular focus in plasma technology. These were great experiences in which I learned many useful technical skills all the way through the design and manufacturing process. Other relevant experience that I have includes work in a research lab run by Prof. Mark Niedre at Northeastern Univerisity know as the Biomedical Optics Research Group. Here I worked under a PHD student to help with with there research into blood analysis through the use of lasers.
